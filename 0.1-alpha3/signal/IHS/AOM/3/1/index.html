This page describes a signal of selection found in the Angola An. coluzzii populationusing the IHS (Cite et al. 20XX) statistic.The focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3R between positions 43,740,000 and 44,560,000. The evidence supporting this signal is weak ( < 50 on one or both flanks).

Signal location. Blue markers show the values of the selection statistic. The dashed black line shows the fitted peak model. The shaded red area shows the focus of the selection signal. The shaded blue area shows the genomic region in linkage with the selection event.
