Burkina Faso An. coluzzii | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #3¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. coluzzii population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is between 3R:52,840,001 and 3L:260,000.

The following 10 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP010301, AGAP010302, AGAP010303 (autophagy-related protein 7), AGAP010304, AGAP010305 (WD repeat and SOF domain-containing protein 1), AGAP010306, AGAP010307 (Pleckstrin homology domain containing, family F (with FYVE domain) member 2), AGAP010308 (nucleolar complex protein 3), AGAP010309, AGAP027986.

The following 2 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP010299, AGAP010300.
