Burkina Faso An. coluzzii | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #4¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. coluzzii population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3L between positions 1,880,001 and 2,160,000.

The following 15 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP010343 (CTL-like protein 2), AGAP010344 (solute carrier family 26, other), AGAP010345 (OTU domain-containing protein 7), AGAP010346 (Mer - merlin (moesin-ezrin-radixin-like protein)), AGAP010347 (CuSOD3 - copper-zinc superoxide dismutase 3), AGAP010348, AGAP010349, AGAP010350, AGAP010351 (insulysin), AGAP010352 (DNA/RNA-binding protein KIN17), AGAP010353 (DNA cross-link repair 1A protein), AGAP010354 (osiris 21), AGAP010355, AGAP010356 (Ring finger protein 130), AGAP010357.

The following 2 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP010358 (paired box protein 3/7), AGAP010359 (Pairberry).
