Angola An. coluzzii | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #4¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Angola An. coluzzii population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3L between positions 17,740,001 and 17,780,000.

Gene AGAP011134 (LIM homeobox protein 5) overlaps the focal region.

The following 8 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP011131 (F-type H -transporting ATPase subunit d), AGAP011132 (GINS complex subunit 3), AGAP011133 (Inosine-5’-monophosphate dehydrogenase), AGAP011135 (synaptosomal-associated protein, 29kDa), AGAP011136, AGAP011137 (Thiomorpholine-carboxylate dehydrogenase), AGAP011138 (myosin XVIII), AGAP011139.
