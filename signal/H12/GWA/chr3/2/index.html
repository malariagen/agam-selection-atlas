Guinea-Bissau | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #2¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Guinea Bissau population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3L between positions 25,080,001 and 25,120,000.

Gene AGAP011480 overlaps the focal region.

The following 4 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP011475 (Envelysin), AGAP011477 (eupolytin), AGAP011478, AGAP011479 (hyaluronoglucosaminidase).
