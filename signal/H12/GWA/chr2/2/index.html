Guinea-Bissau | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #2¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Guinea Bissau population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2R between positions 47,820,001 and 47,860,000.

The following 5 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP004005, AGAP013019, AGAP004006, AGAP004007, AGAP004008.

The following 14 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP004000 (myosin IX), AGAP004002 (60 kDa heat shock protein, mitochondrial precursor), AGAP013015 (p53 and DNA damage-regulated protein), AGAP004003, AGAP004004, AGAP004011 (survival motor neuron protein), AGAP004012 (katanin p60 ATPase-containing subunit), AGAP004013 (SP11838), AGAP004014 (SP11706), AGAP004015 (SP21408), AGAP013186, AGAP004017, AGAP004016, AGAP004018.
