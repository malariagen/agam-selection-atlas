Burkina Faso An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #6¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L between position 27,740,001 and 27,960,000.

The following 13 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP006176, AGAP006177, AGAP006178 (troponin C), AGAP006179 (troponin C), AGAP006180, AGAP006181 (troponin C), AGAP006182 (troponin C), AGAP006183 (slit protein), AGAP006184, AGAP006185, AGAP006186 (Ca-P60A - Calcium-transporting ATPase sarcoplasmic/endoplasmic reticulum type), AGAP006187 (Protein G12), AGAP006188.

No genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region.
