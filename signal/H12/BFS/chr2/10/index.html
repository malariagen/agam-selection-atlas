Burkina Faso An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #10¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2R between position 27,480,001 and 27,520,000.

No genes overlap the focal region.

Gene AGAP002793 (slit homolog 1 protein precursor) is within 50 kbp of the focal region.
