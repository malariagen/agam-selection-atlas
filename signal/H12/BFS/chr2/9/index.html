Burkina Faso An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #9¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2R between position 28,740,001 and 29,160,000.
