Burkina Faso An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #8¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2R between position 61,240,001 and 61,340,000.

The following 2 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP004672, AGAP004674 (Phenoloxidase inhibitor protein).

Gene AGAP004671 (nuclear pore complex protein Nup160) is within 50 kbp of the focal region.
