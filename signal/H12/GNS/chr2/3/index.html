Guinea An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #3¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Guinea An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L between positions 28,740,001 and 28,840,000.

The following 9 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP006260 (Z band alternatively spliced PDZ-motif protein 66), AGAP006261 (CPR135 - cuticular protein RR-2 family 135), AGAP006262, AGAP006263 (ARR2 - arrestin Arr2-like), AGAP006264 (Peroxisomal targeting signal 2 receptor), AGAP006265, AGAP006266 (HIV Tat-specific factor 1), AGAP006267 (CTL6 - C-type lectin (CTL)), AGAP006268.

The following 2 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP006258 (PPO2 - prophenoloxidase 2), AGAP006259.
