Gabon An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #1¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Gabon An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3L between positions 9,740,001 and 10,020,000.

The following 16 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP010748, AGAP010750 (ryanodine receptor 2), AGAP010751, AGAP010752, AGAP010753, AGAP010754, AGAP010755 (protein mago nashi), AGAP010756, AGAP010757, AGAP010758 (Metalloendopeptidase (Fragment)), AGAP010759, AGAP010760, AGAP010761, AGAP010762, AGAP010763, AGAP010764 (Metalloendopeptidase).

The following 5 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP010745, AGAP010746, AGAP010765, AGAP010766, AGAP010767.
