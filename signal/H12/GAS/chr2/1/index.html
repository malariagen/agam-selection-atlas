Gabon An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #1¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Gabon An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L between positions 25,460,001 and 25,540,000.

The following 10 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP006030 (mfrn - mitoferrin), AGAP006031 (nuclear pore complex protein Nup54), AGAP006032, AGAP006033, AGAP006034, AGAP006035 (Ras-related protein Rab-36), AGAP006036 (axonemal dynein intermediate chain inner arm i1), AGAP006037 (RpL24 - 60S ribosomal protein L24), AGAP006038 (serine/arginine repetitive matrix protein 2), AGAP006039.

The following 8 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP006028 (Rdl - GABA-gated chloride channel subunit), AGAP006029, AGAP006040 (peroxisomal membrane protein 2), AGAP006041 (E3 ubiquitin-protein ligase RNF5), AGAP006042, AGAP029130, AGAP029102, AGAP006045 (protein yorkie).
