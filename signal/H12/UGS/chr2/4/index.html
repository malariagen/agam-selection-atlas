Uganda An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 2 | Signal #4¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Uganda An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L between positions 8,600,001 and 8,780,000.

The following 13 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP005031 (E3 SUMO-protein ligase PIAS2), AGAP005032 (calnexin), AGAP005033 (U3 small nucleolar RNA-associated protein 20), AGAP005034 (GPRMGL5 - putative metabotropic glutamate receptor 5), AGAP005035 (DNA helicase INO80), AGAP005036, AGAP005037 (Coiled-coil domain-containing protein AGAP005037), AGAP005038, AGAP005039, AGAP005040 (RILP-like protein-like protein), AGAP005041, AGAP005042, AGAP005043 (Inactive dipeptidyl peptidase 10).

The following 13 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP005029 (serine-threonine kinase receptor-associated protein), AGAP005030, AGAP005044, AGAP005045, AGAP005046 (RpL22 - 60S ribosomal protein L22), AGAP005047 (Gr43 - gustatory receptor 43), AGAP028210 (SRPN13 - serine protease inhibitor (serpin) 13), AGAP005048 (anaphase-promoting complex subunit 2), AGAP005049 (coiled-coil domain-containing protein 53), AGAP005050 (interferon-stimulated gene 20 kDa protein), AGAP005051 (DNA polymerase epsilon subunit 3), AGAP005052 (deoxyribodipyrimidine photo-lyase), AGAP005053 (E3 ubiquitin-protein ligase LRSAM1).
