Uganda An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #4¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Uganda An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3R between positions 18,920,001 and 18,980,000.
