Uganda An. gambiae | H12 | Chromosome 3 | Signal #2¶

This page describes a signal of selection found in the Uganda An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3R between positions 49,380,001 and 49,720,000.

The following 20 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP010142 (Dat - dopamine N-acetyltransferase), AGAP010143, AGAP010144, AGAP010145 (yellow), AGAP010146, AGAP010147 (myosin heavy chain), AGAP010148, AGAP010149 (cytochrome b5-related), AGAP010150 (cytochrome b5-related), AGAP010151, AGAP010152 (gem associated protein 5), AGAP010155, AGAP013739, AGAP010156 (ATP citrate lyase), AGAP013762, AGAP010157 (Ast2 - allatostatin 2), AGAP010158, AGAP010159 (dUTP pyrophosphatase), AGAP010160 (myosin I), AGAP010161.

The following 5 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal region: AGAP010138 (uncharacterized protein yjbQ), AGAP010139 (GMP synthase (glutamine-hydrolysing)), AGAP010140 (hydrolases of HD superfamily), AGAP010141 (DnaJ homolog subfamily C member 4), AGAP010162.
