This page describes a signal of selection found in the Angola An. coluzzii population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3L between positions 17,740,001 and 17,780,000. The evidence supporting this signal is moderate ( >= 50 on both flanks).

Signal location. Blue markers show the values of the selection statistic. The dashed black line shows the fitted peak model. The shaded red area shows the focus of the selection signal. The shaded blue area shows the genomic region in linkage with the selection event.
