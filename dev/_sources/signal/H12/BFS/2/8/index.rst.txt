H12 / Burkina Faso *An. gambiae* / Chromosome 2 / #8
====================================================

This page describes a signal of selection found in the
:doc:`../../../../../population/BFS` population using the
:doc:`../../../../../method/H12` statistic.The focus of this signal is on chromosome arm
**2L** between positions **42,300,001** and
**42,380,000**.
The evidence supporting this signal is
**weak** (:math:`\Delta_{i}` < 50 on one or both flanks).

Genes
-----



Gene :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007086`:sup:`2` (Sodium channel protein) overlaps the focal region.





The following 8 genes are within 50 kbp of the focal
region: :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007081`,  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007082`:sup:`1` (NADH dehydrogenase (ubiquinone) Fe-S protein 4),  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007083`,  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007084`,  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007085`,  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007087` (RpL41 - 60s ribosomal protein L41),  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007088` (peptidyl-prolyl cis-trans isomerase B (cyclophilin B)),  :doc:`../../../../../gene/AGAP007089`.


Key to insecticide resistance candidate gene types: :sup:`1` metabolic;
:sup:`2` target-site; :sup:`3` behavioural; :sup:`4` cuticular.
