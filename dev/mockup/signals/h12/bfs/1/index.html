    Burkina Faso An. gambiae H12 #1¶

    This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso Anopheles gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.

    The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L from position 2,420,000 to 2,460,000. The following 3 genes overlap the focal region: AGAP001234, AGAP001234, AGAP001234. The following 5 genes are in windows immediately adjacent to the focal region: AGAP001234, AGAP001234, AGAP001234, AGAP001234, AGAP001234.
