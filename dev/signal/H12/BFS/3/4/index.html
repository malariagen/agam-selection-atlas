This page describes a signal of selection found in the Burkina Faso An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 3R between positions 31,760,001 and 31,800,000. The evidence supporting this signal is weak ( < 50 on one or both flanks).

Signal location. Blue markers show the values of the selection statistic. The dashed black line shows the fitted peak model. The shaded red area shows the focus of the selection signal. The shaded blue area shows the genomic region in linkage with the selection event.
