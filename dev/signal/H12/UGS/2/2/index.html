This page describes a signal of selection found in the Uganda An. gambiae population using the H12 (Garud et al. 20XX) statistic.The inferred focus of this signal is on chromosome arm 2L between positions 2,520,001 and 3,120,000. The evidence supporting this signal is strong ( >= 100 on both flanks).
